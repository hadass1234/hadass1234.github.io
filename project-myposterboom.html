#myposterboom — 2020

ART DIRECTION GRAPHIC DESIGN SOCIAL MEDIA

#myposterboom is a 30-days ongoing personal project of poster design.
During the design process I explored the space of breaking common design rules of layout, typography and composition, which resulted in unique outcomes. My strongest inspirations were contemporary and indie poster design, as well as daily events.
