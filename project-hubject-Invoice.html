UX UI B2B SAAS FINANCE

I designed the end-to-end invoice management experience for a new B2B financial services product.
The new system solution automates invoices creation, directly connected to charging data, and simplifies disputes handling for users.

Hubject Financial Services offers a platform and service that helps EV charging companies manage B2B invoices, payments collection and distribution, and financial data. It streamlines processes, ensures compliance with regulations, and reduces errors in billing.

üí° Key insight

I discovered users place a strong emphasis on data verification, given the legal nature of invoices. After uncovering this core users need, I began drafting the information structure for the invoice view, focusing on clarity, accuracy, and traceability to support the verification process.

üöÄ The solution

We decided to focus on automation to reduce manual work and minimize errors, while keeping key manual touchpoints where users can review and confirm the ongoing workflow. This approach balances efficiency with user control.

The final design solution is a centralized invoice view that automates data handling, ensures clarity, and supports human edge cases.

1Ô∏è‚É£ Key solution - All-in-One invoice view

By centralizing all invoicing related data, the process becomes faster, more accurate, and less dependent on back-and-forth between teams.

Financial managers can now:

  1. Easily track invoice status and key details: with the invoices overview categorized by statuses.
  2. Quickly access original charging session data: resolve questions and disputes faster without relying on other departments.
  3. Validate B2B client information directly in the invoice view: fix billing data issues immediately, saving time and preventing costly mistakes.
  4. Export invoice data in multiple formats (CSV, PDF, JSON): to easily integrate with external systems for smoother operations.
