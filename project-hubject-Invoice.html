I designed the end-to-end invoice management workflow for a new B2B financial services product.
The system solution automated invoices creation, ensured data accuracy, and supported easy payments and payouts, while meeting regulatory compliance requirements.

Financial challenges in the EV Industry

B2B sellers in the EV charging space faced conflicts with B2B clients over inaccurate invoices, leading to payment delays and revenue loss. Clients disputed invoice amounts, requiring proof of data accuracy before paying, which slowed down the invoicing process and disrupted cash flow.


The Problem

B2B Seller companies faced challenges when managing invoices:
  •   Manual, error-prone processes to verify billing data
  •   Lack of transparency into how invoice amounts were calculated
  •   Time-consuming workflows for approvals and dispute handling

These inefficiencies slowed down monthly operations and created friction between finance and energy managers.

The original Hubject eRoaming welcome page - EMP user

The problem

The welcome page was failing to deliver value to users. It featured only 4-5 static widgets that provided minimal new information, leaving users frustrated when attempting to navigate the portal effectively. Additionally, the design lacked alignment with the product's design system.

CPO and EMP eRoaming managers struggle to efficiently address urgent matters (like expiring contracts, charging issues or reaching out to partners), due to unclear navigation and a low-value welcome page.

Our hypothesis was that replacing the welcome page with a dashboard could provide users with more actionable value and improve their overall experience.
