STEPHEN WILKS — 2021
Website redesign

ART DIRECTION INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE RESPONSIVE UI DESIGN HTML

Stephen Wilks is a Berlin-based artist, sculptor, and photographer. His work revolves around kabbalah and conceptual subjects.

During my talks with Wilks, we narrowed down his wishes for the future design: a polished display of his work, easier navigation across projects, and mobile-friendly.
The design and composition of the website are a visual extension of the artist’s core values: poetic, spiritual, and natural. It has been at the center of my decision-making process to create an online platform that will resonate and compliment Wilks’ work and craft.

Full project online: stephenwilks.net

The Website

After understanding Wilks’ artistic value, his needs, and a competitor's research, I structured the core flow of his website:

  1. Home page - first display
  2. Work page - all work library
  3. Info page
  4. Shop page

I made an artistic decision to separate the home page from the work page, in order to provide a proper first-impression of the work, and only later the navigation.

First page is dedicated to displaying Wilks’ work in an exhibition-like view. Each work previewed with a slow transition, and a lot of white space.
