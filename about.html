About

I'm Hadass, a Product & Graphic Designer, originally from Israel, based in Berlin.

I'm passionate about creating contemporary, thoughtful, and yet practical design, with a particular interest in human-centric experience and branded visual concept with strong focus on digital products. In my work, I enjoy creating meaningful design solutions with real purpose and value.

In my free time I love writing and art, visiting museums around the world, and learning to design using HTML - coding this website is my playground.
