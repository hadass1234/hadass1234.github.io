About
I'm Hadass, a Digital Product Designer (UI/UX) based in Berlin.

My Mission
As a product designer, I'm driven by the desire to make a meaningful impact through my work. My mission is to create designs that not only solve real-world problems but also resonate deeply with end users. I believe in the power of thoughtful design to drive positive change and achieve both user's and business goals.

Design background
With a solid education in visual communication, I've built my design career through years of experience in creative agencies and tech environments. I'm proactive, and strategic, and enjoy collaborating with teams to bring impact and success. This combination of design background and real-life experience has shaped my skills and today allows me to create design solutions that are both impactful and visually appealing.
