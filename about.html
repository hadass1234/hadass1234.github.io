About
I'm Hadass, a Digital Product Designer (UI/UX) based in Berlin, with a strong focus on creating impact through high-quality design work.

My Mission
As a product designer, I'm driven by the desire to make a meaningful impact through my work. My mission is to create designs that not only solve real-world problems but also resonate deeply with end users. I believe in the power of thoughtful design to drive positive change and achieve both user's and business goals.

My background
I started my creative career with a solid visual communication education in Berlin, and I've built my design career through years of experience in creative agencies and tech environments. The combination of design background and real-life experience has strongly shaped my skills and today allows me to create design solutions that are both impactful and visually appealing.

Strengths & Philosophy

●    Prototyping and Interface Design

It's all about great design.
I excel in creating seamless prototypes and pixel-perfect interfaces, combining my meticulous design skills with a keen eye for detail to deliver consistent, user-friendly products that enhance the user experience.

●    Holistic Product Mindset

Think twice - design once.
Strategic refinement is the foundation of efficient execution. I adopt a big-picture approach in every project, ensuring each design decision contributes to a cohesive user experience. This holistic mindset accelerates development and results in scalable, impactful product solutions.

●    User-Centered Problem Solving

I stand for win-win-win solutions.
Every challenge is an opportunity to craft user-centered designs that not only meet business goals but exceed them. I combine pragmatic problem-solving with creativity, addressing pain points with impactful, strategic solutions.

●    Empathy and Communication

Communication is everything.
I thrive in collaborative environments, valuing diverse perspectives and fostering connections. I engage actively with stakeholders, from users to developers, ensuring alignment throughout the design process to create exceptional products.
