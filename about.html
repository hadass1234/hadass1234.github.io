About
I’m Hadass, a Digital Product Designer (UX/UI) based in Berlin.

My Mission
As a product designer, I'm driven by the desire to make a meaningful impact through my work. My mission is to create designs that not only solve real-world problems but also resonate deeply with end users. I believe in the power of thoughtful design to drive positive change and achieve both user's and business goals.

My Professional Strength
With a strong background in visual design and extensive hands-on professional experience, I bring a unique blend of creativity and conceptual thinking to every project. From designing new features to optimizing existing products, I contribute throughout the product lifecycle step by step. Proactive, collaborative, and detail-oriented, I approach each project with a focus on the big picture and a commitment to delivering high-quality, user-centric solutions.
