toshi — 2022
Invest with every shopping

USER EXPERIENCE STRATEGY PROTOTYPING ART DIRECTION INTERFACE DESIGN

toshi is a German based startup that helps users invest money for their retirement. With toshi's automated app, a certain precentage for every cashless shopping the user makes, will be invested in a long-term portfolio.

The challenge: redesign an investment portfolio dashboard which has all necessary information and is easy to understand for people who did not invest before.

🏆 First place winning project in Ironhack Germany hackshow DEC 2022 (UX/UI category)

Responsibilities: Research, UX Design, Strategy, Prototyping, Art Direction, Graphic Design.
Credits: Ironhack x toshi. Co-Team: Amanda Liedberg
