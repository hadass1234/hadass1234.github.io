Judaikit is a series of modern branded judaica (jewish ceremonial art) packaged kits. Traditionally, judaica is a variety of objects used by jewish community for religious ritual purposes.

Considering the overall society shift into a more inclusive mindset, globalizations and rise of self-search, Judaikit is proposing an innovative solution for a new generation of jewish individuals, seeking to integrate their cultural roots with their fast-paced and modern lifestyles.

Full project online: judaikit.online

The brand consists of 3 main lines:

  1. Basic line - for weekly/daily use
  2. Holidays line - for yearly occasions
  3. Life Occasions line - for special events during a person’s life

Every line has distinct visual characteristics, while each individual kit is tailored made for the Jewish ritual it is dedicated for. The objects are delivered in a minimal and symbolic packaging design, which are all influenced by the common colour pallete and context of each ritual.

The main target group consists of the young jewish immigrant generation, mostly originating from the largest Jewish communities in either Israel or the US.
In order successfully connect with all targeted users, it is rather necessary to communicate in both Hebrew and English.

The bilingual need created a design challenge, which eventually I was able to solve by using a wide, balanced layout throughout the packaging and info booklet. The website as well is available in both Hebrew and English versions
