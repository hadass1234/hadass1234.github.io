One Warm Winter x Highsnobiety — 2021
GOOD XSAMPLE 2.0 Charity Market

VISUAL CONCEPT GRAPHIC DESIGN LOGO SOCIAL MEDIA

GOOD XSAMPLE is a charity market event, all profits donatied to help the homeless in Berlin.
The event is a collaboration between One Warm Winter (a Berlin-based honelessness charity) and Highsnobiety (a fashion & lifestyle magazine).
In the event, Highsnobiety offers for sale exclusive art, design and cult fashion samples, all contributed out of their archive.

The visual language for the event was inspired by the concept of fashion and art, depicting abstract shaped fashion items colored in the One Warm Winter light blue shade for a clear connection to the cause.

My responsibilities: Visual concept development, Graphic design
Team: 1 Creative director, 1 Graphic designer, 1 Motion designer
Agency: DOJO Berlin
