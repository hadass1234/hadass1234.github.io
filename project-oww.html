One Warm Winter x Highsnobiety: GOOD XSAMPLE Market — 2021
ART DIRECTION GRAPHIC DESIGN LOGO SOCIAL MEDIA

GOOD XSAMPLE 2.0 is a charit market collaboration between the berlin-based One Warm Winter homelessness foundation, and Highsnobiety, a well-known fashion and lifestyle blog.

In the event, Highsnobiety featured out of their archive exclusive art, design and cult fashion samples for sale, with all profits donated to help the homeless in Berlin.

The visual language for the event was inspired by the concept of fashion and art, depicting abstract shapes of fashion items colored in the One Warm Winter light blue shade for a clear connection to the cause.

Responsibilities: Visual concept development, Graphic design.
Agency: DOJO Berlin
