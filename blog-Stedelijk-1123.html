My trip to the Netherlands

It turns¬†out that museums in Amsterdam are quite expensive. Knowing a museum experience can be quite energy-consuming for me, I decided to visit only one museum this time. Being the big contemporary fan that I am (and I already visited the Van Gogh Museum a few years ago), I went to the Stedelijk Museum.

I traveled during complicated times. Right next to the museum, in the Museumplein,¬†there was a huge climate change protest that somehow turned into a pro-Palestinian protest.
