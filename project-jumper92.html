JUMPER 92 — 2018
BRANDING GRAPHIC DESIGN PHOTOGRAPHY PRINT UI/UX

JUMPER 92 is a vintage jumpers store, originated in Berlin. The store offers a unique range of patterned jumpers that smoothly fits in Berlin's fashion scene.

The brand’s visual language evolved from the 90’s retro culture into a contemporary and refined 360° fashion product: starting with the logo - a minimalistic take on the sega-pixel art, to a fully designed stationary system based on winter-associated colours, all the way to feature studio photography and trendy app design.

The aim of the JUMPER 92 app is to create a digital shopping experience alongside the real store.
In order to imitate the vintage shopping experience, the app has both inspirational-creative aspects alongside the purchase e-commerce experience.

The Inspiration part is influenced by social media flow, using 'story' and 'post' features with sharing options.
The shopping part is using classical ecommerce flow, from easy browsing, item focus to easy payment.
