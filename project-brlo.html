BRLO: OOH Ad campaign — 2021
Advertising, Print

First Out-Of-Home (OOH) advertising campaign for BRLO - a Berlin based craft brewery.
A strong focus on the black & white blocky look of BRLO, combined with brush strokes associated with craft and rebel spirit. In addition, a big emphasis was placed on the bottles and the label design elements.


Responsibilities: Visual concept development, Graphic design.
Agency: DOJO Berlin
