Cum to Sleep — 2022
Erotic sleeping app

UX UI B2C LIFESTYLE IOS APP

The new wellness app solves sleeping problems using erotic-based content and anonymous chat platform.
Based on the scientific findings that erotic pleasure results stress relief, the app's flow can effectively lead users to a long-term restful sleep solution.

Cum to Sleep is a new app idea for a student project - addressing a fictional brief, client's business needs, and real users' problem.
