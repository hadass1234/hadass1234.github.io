Cum to Sleep — 2022
Erotic sleeping app

DESIGN THINKING UX DESIGN iOS ART DIRECTION UI DESIGN

Cum to Sleep is a wellness app, that solves sleeping problems using erotic-based content.  Based on the leading insight that stress is the top reason for sleeping problems, we developed an app based on science that sexual experience can eventually lead to stress relief. 
The main feature of Cum to Sleep is anonymous sex chat, in which users can create avatars, chat with other avatars, and reach erotic experiences through fantasy in the safety of their homes.



Responsibilities: UX Research, UX Design, Strategy, Prototyping, Art Direction, UI Design
Credits: Project partner: Lucas Pinto, Ironhack Berlin

The Brief and Client


The Daily Health Conference is a non-profit (fictional) organization dedicated to promoting health and wellness. Even though The Daily Health Conference has numerous years of experience in the field, they have seen a substantial drop in subscription memberships.
As a result, the Daily Health Conference wants to find a way to offer more value to its members and in particular, they are excited to explore how technology can be used to help people live healthier lives.

The client wishes to develop a native app that will address and help to improve an aspect of personal well-being and retains GDPR compliance. Our team’s choice is to focus on solving sleeping problems. 

The Challenge


How can we design an app that will address and help users solve their sleeping problems?
In addition, how can we create an app that will stand out, and serve the client’s goal to increase users’ engagement and increase subscribers?

The Strategy


We approached the challenge using Design Thinking methods. Started with the research phase, including Secondary research, Interviews, Competitor analysis, and surveys. The research generated a lot of opportunities and understanding of the market, which is why we focused on the quantitative aspect and focused on personas and user journeys based on common patterns. 

Through spontaneous and playful ideation sitting, we discovered an untouched potential area in the market - sex and masturbation as a way to relieve stress and help fall asleep. We explored options based on the persona and interviews and came up with an up that will lead the users to fall asleep in a more efficient way, in a long-term potential.

The Results


For the app MVP presentation, we focused on the most original flow: Anonymus chat that allows erotic sexting through avatar use. The flow is addressing the main persona, knowing there is no need to meet the partner, rather creating an erotic fantasy that will lead the user to sexual arousal, and then sex reduction. The Cum to Sleep app design is considering sleeping conditions, which is why it is ruled by dark screens, The colors are inspired by emojis associated with sexting, and the app elements are communicating elegance and erotica.
